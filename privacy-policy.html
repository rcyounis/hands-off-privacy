HandsOFF Guardian Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2024

1. Introduction

HandsOFF Guardian ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our macOS application.

2. Information We Collect

Our application uses your device's camera to detect hand movements. This processing is done locally on your device, and we do not collect, store, or transmit any video or image data.

We collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app's performance and user experience. This includes:

  • Number of alerts triggered
  • App usage duration
  • Feature usage patterns

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Improve app performance and accuracy
  • Enhance user experience
  • Provide personalized insights

4. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit any personal data to external servers.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data stored in the app
  • Delete your data at any time
  • Opt out of anonymous usage statistics

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
